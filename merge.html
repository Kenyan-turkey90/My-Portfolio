About Me

Chris Kariuki Macharia

I am a passionate Web Developer with expertise in both front-end and back-end development, crafting seamless, dynamic, and responsive digital experiences. With a strong foundation in modern web technologies, I specialize in building intuitive interfaces, optimizing performance, and delivering user-centric solutions.

Beyond web development, I am a professional Graphic Designer, proficient in industry-leading tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Lightroom. Whether it’s branding, digital illustrations, or editorial designs, I bring creative visions to life with precision, color theory, and visual storytelling.

My skills extend into video editing and motion graphics, where I work with powerful platforms like DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and After Effects to produce high-quality cinematic visuals. From dynamic edits to compelling animations, I craft content that resonates with audiences.

Additionally, I am an experienced 3D Artist, harnessing the capabilities of Autodesk Maya, Blender, and 3ds Max to create realistic models, environments, and animations. Whether it's character modeling, architectural visualization, or dynamic animations, I bring imagination into reality with advanced 3D techniques.
